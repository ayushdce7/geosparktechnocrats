Who We Are

A New Firm, Built on Experienced Foundations

GeoSpark Technocrats is a multi-disciplinary consultancy and construction firm delivering integrated solutions that combine expert advisory services with efficient project execution. We bring together engineering, construction and infrastructure capability to provide end-to-end services that ensure quality, compliance and client satisfaction.

Our capabilities span geological, geophysical, hydrological, topographical, environmental, socio-economic, LiDAR and drone surveys; DPR preparation and design consultancy for railways, highways, tunnels, bridges and buildings; and hydraulic design for dams, canals, flood modelling and water supply systems.

We are also engaged in disaster management, mining plans, renewable energy initiatives and specialised training in GIS, remote sensing and water conservation — guided always by innovation, sustainability and a client-centric approach.
